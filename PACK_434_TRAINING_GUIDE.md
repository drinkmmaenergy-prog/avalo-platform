## 1. Ambassador Basics

### What is an Ambassador?

An Avalo Ambassador is a local growth driver who:
- Recruits new users through offline channels
- Hosts events and community meetups
- Partners with local businesses
- Recruits creators for the platform
- Represents Avalo in their region

### Ambassador Roles

#### City Ambassador
- **Territory**: Entire city or metropolitan area
- **Focus**: Large-scale growth, creator partnerships, major events
- **KPIs**: 100+ monthly referrals, 10+ creators, 3+ events per quarter

#### Campus Ambassador
- **Territory**: University campus and surroundings
- **Focus**: Student recruitment, campus events, organization partnerships
- **KPIs**: 50+ monthly referrals, 5+ creators, 2+ events per quarter

#### Nightlife Ambassador
- **Territory**: Nightlife venues in city
- **Focus**: Club partnerships, nightlife creators, party events
- **KPIs**: 80+ monthly referrals, 8+ creators, 4+ events per quarter

#### Creator Recruiter
- **Territory**: Regional (multiple cities)
- **Focus**: High-value creator acquisition, content strategy
- **KPIs**: 30+ monthly referrals, 15+ creators recruited

#### Community Ambassador
- **Territory**: Local neighborhood or district
- **Focus**: Community building, local events, grassroots growth
- **KPIs**: 60+ monthly referrals, 6+ creators, 3+ events per quarter

### Tier System

Your tier determines your compensation multiplier:

- **Bronze** (1.0x): Starting tier
- **Silver** (1.3x): 50+ referrals, 5+ creators, $500+ revenue
- **Gold** (1.6x): 200+ referrals, 20+ creators, $2,000+ revenue
- **Platinum** (2.0x): 500+ referrals, 50+ creators, $5,000+ revenue
- **Titan** (3.0x): 1,000+ referrals, 100+ creators, $10,000+ revenue

### Compensation Models

1. **CPI (Cost Per Install)**: Earn for each app download via your code
2. **CPA (Cost Per Activation)**: Bonus when user completes KYC + profile
3. **CPS (Cost Per Subscriber)**: Commission when referral buys Royal/VIP
4. **RevShare**: 1-3% of referred user's lifetime spending (12 months)
5. **Event Rewards**: Per-attendee payments for successful events
6. **Tier Bonuses**: Promotion bonuses and multipliers

## 2. How to Host an Avalo Event

### Event Planning

#### 1. Choose Your Event Type

- **Meetup**: Casual gathering for Avalo users
- **Speed Dating**: Structured dating event
- **Creator Night**: Featuring platform creators
- **Launch Party**: Grand opening in new area
- **Workshop**: Training or skill-building event
- **Tournament**: Competitive gaming or challenges

#### 2. Select Venue

Good venues:
- Nightclubs and bars
- Coworking spaces
- University common areas
- Parks and public spaces
- Restaurants with event space
- Fitness centers

Criteria:
- Central location
- Good WiFi
- Space for 30-200 people
- Permission to promote Avalo
- QR code placement options

#### 3. Set Date & Time

Best times:
- Weekends (Friday/Saturday evening)
- University events (Thursday evenings)
- Happy hours (5-8 PM weekdays)
- Special occasions (holidays, season openings)

Give at least 2 weeks notice!

#### 4. Promote Your Event

Channels:
- Your Avalo ambassador QR codes
- Social media posts
- Campus flyers and posters
- Partner venue promotion
- WhatsApp groups
- Influencer partnerships

Create urgency with:
- Early bird tickets
- Limited capacity
- Exclusive perks
- Token giveaways

### Event Execution

#### Day Before:
- [ ] Confirm venue booking
- [ ] Test QR codes
- [ ] Prepare check-in system
- [ ] Brief venue staff
- [ ] Charge all devices
- [ ] Print backup QR codes

#### Day Of:
- [ ] Arrive 1 hour early
- [ ] Set up check-in area
- [ ] Test WiFi
- [ ] Place QR codes strategically
- [ ] Prepare welcome materials
- [ ] Designate Avalo ambassadors/helpers

#### During Event:
- [ ] Greet all attendees personally
- [ ] Encourage app downloads
- [ ] Guide through registration
- [ ] Facilitate connections
- [ ] Take photos/videos for content
- [ ] Monitor attendance numbers
- [ ] Handle any issues

#### After Event:
- [ ] Thank venue and partners
- [ ] Follow up with attendees
- [ ] Submit event report
- [ ] Share photos/content
- [ ] Process check-ins for attribution
- [ ] Plan follow-up event

### Event Check-In System

1. **QR Code Scan**: Attendees scan your event-specific QR
2. **Geo-Validation**: System confirms they're at venue
3. **Time Window**: Check-in valid 1 hour before to 2 hours after
4. **Attribution**: If they sign up, you get credit
5. **Rewards**: Earn per verified attendee

**Anti-Fraud**: System detects fake GPS, VPN usage, and duplicate scans.

## 3. Safety Protocols

### Personal Safety

1. **Meet in Public**: Always host events in public venues
2. **Bring Support**: Have another ambassador or friend help
3. **Share Location**: Tell someone where you'll be
4. **Trust Your Instincts**: Leave if situation feels unsafe
5. **Emergency Contacts**: Have local emergency numbers ready

### User Safety

1. **Age Verification**: Only recruit users 18+ (21+ for nightlife)
2. **No Harassment**: Zero tolerance for inappropriate behavior
3. **Safe Spaces**: Events should be welcoming to all
4. **Consent Culture**: Promote respectful interactions
5. **Report Issues**: Flag any concerning behavior immediately

### Platform Safety

1. **Authentic Recruitment**: Only recruit real people
2. **No Fake Accounts**: Never create fake profiles
3. **Honest Marketing**: Accurately represent Avalo
4. **Data Privacy**: Never share user information
5. **Anti-Fraud**: Follow geo-validation requirements

### What to Report

Report immediately if you encounter:
- Fake profiles or bot accounts
- Harassment or abuse
- Underage users
- Fraud attempts
- Safety concerns
- Venue issues
- Partner violations

**Emergency Contact**: safety@avalo.app

## 4. Creator Recruiting

### Identifying Potential Creators

Look for:
- **Social Media Following**: 1,000+ followers
- **Engagement**: Active audience interaction
- **Content Quality**: Good production value
- **Authenticity**: Genuine personality
- **Niche**: Fits Avalo categories

### Recruitment Approach

#### 1. Research
- Review their content
- Check engagement rates
- Understand their brand
- Identify value proposition

#### 2. Reach Out
- DM on their main platform
- Professional introduction
- Mention specific content you liked
- Brief Avalo pitch
- Offer to meet in person

#### 3. Pitch Avalo Creator Benefits
- Monetization opportunities
- Fan club system
- No platform fees (vs 30-50% elsewhere)
- Direct fan relationships
- Multi-revenue streams:
  - Subscriptions
  - Tips
  - Paid messages
  - Exclusive content
  - Meet & greets

#### 4. Demo the Platform
- Show creator dashboard
- Walk through fan club setup
- Explain payout system
- Share success stories
- Answer questions

#### 5. Onboard
- Guide through creator application
- Help set up profile
- Assist with first posts
- Introduce to other creators
- Provide ongoing support

### Creator Types to Target

1. **Micro-Influencers**: 1K-50K followers, high engagement
2. **Local Celebrities**: Known in your city
3. **Fitness Trainers**: Classes, workouts, nutrition
4. **Musicians/DJs**: Local music scene
5. **Artists**: Visual artists, photographers
6. **Comedians**: Stand-up, sketch comedy
7. **Educators**: Tutors, coaches, mentors
8. **Lifestyle**: Fashion, beauty, wellness

### Creator Compensation

You earn when recruited creators:
- Complete profile setup
- Make first $100 in earnings
- Reach subscriber milestones
- Stay active (monthly bonus)

Typical earnings: $20-100 per active creator

## FAQ

**Q: How long does it take to see earnings?**  
A: First payout within 30 days of your first verified conversions.

**Q: What if someone already uses Avalo?**  
A: Referrals only count for new users. Focus on non-users.

**Q: Can I be ambassador in multiple cities?**  
A: Yes, if you can manage the territories effectively. Requires Platinum tier.

**Q: What happens if I violate terms?**  
A: Warnings, earnings freeze, suspension, or termination depending on severity.

**Q: How do I report fraud?**  
A: Email fraud@avalo.app with details and evidence.

**Q: Can I hire assistants?**  
A: Yes! Build your team. They operate under your account.

**Q: What if partner venue violates agreement?**  
A: Report to partnerships@avalo.app immediately.
