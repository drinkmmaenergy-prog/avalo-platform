## üé® CREATIVE GUIDELINES

### Headlines Must:
- Command attention immediately
- Use active, present tense
- Be under 10 words
- Create curiosity or desire
- Never apologize or hedge

### Body Copy Must:
- Lead with benefit, not feature
- Use "you" more than "we"
- Short sentences (max 15 words)
- One idea per sentence
- End with clear action

### CTAs Must:
- Tell them exactly what happens next
- Use action verbs (Claim, Unlock, Start, Get, Be)
- Create urgency or desire
- Never be passive (avoid "Learn More", "Find Out")
