## 2. TYPES OF SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 2.1 Creator Subscriptions

**What it is:**  
Monthly recurring subscription to a specific creator's exclusive content and perks.

**Pricing:**  
- Set by individual creators
- Typical range: 500-5,000 tokens/month
- Minimum: 100 tokens/month
- Maximum: 50,000 tokens/month

**Benefits (creator-defined):**
- Exclusive content (photos, videos, stories)
- Subscriber-only posts and updates
- Discounts on products (typically 10-20%)
- Priority messaging (faster response)
- Subscriber-only live streams
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Custom content requests (varies by creator)

**Billing:**  
- Charged monthly on subscription date
- Prorated on first purchase if mid-month start
- Auto-renews unless canceled

### 2.2 Avalo Premium (Platform Subscription)

**What it is:**  
Platform-wide premium membership with enhanced features.

**Pricing:**  
- **Monthly:** 1,000 tokens (~200 PLN)
- **Annual:** 10,000 tokens (~1,900 PLN, save 20%)

**Benefits:**
- Unlimited swipes (no daily limit)
- Advanced filters (income, education, verification status)
- See who liked you
- Rewind last 10 swipes
- 5 Super Likes per day (vs 1 for free users)
- Incognito mode (browse invisibly)
- Priority customer support
- Profile boost (4x per month)
- Read receipts on messages
- No ads

**Note:** Avalo Premium does NOT include free tokens for chat. Messaging still requires token deposits.

### 3.2 Auto-Renewal

**How it works:**
- Subscriptions automatically renew monthly (or annually)
- Renewal charge occurs on subscription anniversary date
- Notification sent 7 days before renewal
- Renewal confirmation sent upon successful charge

**Disabling Auto-Renewal:**
- Can be disabled anytime in Settings > Subscriptions
- Takes effect at end of current billing period
- No partial refunds for early cancellation

---

### 3.3 Failed Payments

**If payment fails:**
1. **Retry:** System attempts charge 3 times over 7 days
2. **Notification:** Email sent after each failed attempt
3. **Grace Period:** 7 days to update payment method
4. **Suspension:** Subscription suspended after grace period
5. **Cancellation:** Permanently canceled after 30 days unpaid

**During Grace Period:**  
Benefits remain active but with warning notice.

**After Suspension:**  
Benefits immediately revoked until payment successful.

---

### 3.4 Price Changes

**Notice:**
- 30 days advance notice for price increases
- Option to cancel before increase takes effect
- Continued subscription = acceptance of new price

**Grandfathering:**  
Existing subscribers may be grandfathered at old price for limited time (creator's discretion).

### 5.2 EU Consumer Rights (14-Day Withdrawal)

Under **EU Directive 2011/83/EU**, you have a **14-day right of withdrawal** from subscription purchase, PROVIDED:
- Subscription service has NOT begun yet, OR
- You have NOT accessed any subscriber-only content

**Waiver of Right:**  
By accessing subscriber content, you waive your 14-day withdrawal right (you explicitly consent to immediate service provision).

**How to Exercise:**
- Email: refunds@avalo.app
- Subject: "EU Withdrawal Right — Subscription"
- Include: User ID, subscription details, purchase date
- Processing: 5-10 business days
