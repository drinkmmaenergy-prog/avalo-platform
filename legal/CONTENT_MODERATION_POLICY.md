# AVALO CONTENT MODERATION POLICY

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025  
**Compliance:** EU DSA, GDPR

## 1. PROHIBITED CONTENT

### Illegal (Zero Tolerance)
- CSAM (immediate law enforcement report)
- Terrorism content
- Human trafficking
- Prostitution/escort solicitation (INSTANT BAN)
- Illegal drugs/weapons
- Copyright infringement

### Harmful
- Violence/gore
- Self-harm promotion
- Hate speech
- Harassment
- Revenge porn
- Doxxing

## 2. MODERATION METHODS

### AI Moderation
- Auto-scan all content
- Risk scores: 80-100=block, 60-79=review, 40-59=warn, 0-39=allow

### User Reports
- 24/7 reporting available
- Priority queue for severe issues
- Response: 24h critical, 72h standard

### Human Review
- Trained moderators
- Final decision authority
- Appeal available

## 3. ENFORCEMENT

### Warning
First minor violation, content removed

### Suspension
7-30 days for repeat violations

### Permanent Ban
- Severe violations
- Three strikes
- Prostitution/escorting (instant, final)

### Legal Action
CSAM, terrorism, trafficking reported to authorities

## 4. APPEALS

Submit to appeals@avalo.app within 14 days. No appeals for CSAM or prostitution.

## 5. TRANSPARENCY

Quarterly reports published per EU DSA Article 15.

**Contact:** moderation@avalo.app
