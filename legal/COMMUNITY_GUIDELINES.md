# AVALO COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## BE RESPECTFUL
- Treat others with kindness
- No harassment or bullying
- Respect boundaries and consent
- No hate speech or discrimination

## BE SAFE
- Meet in public places only
- Never share personal info early
- Report suspicious behavior
- Trust your instincts

## BE HONEST
- Use real photos
- Be truthful in your profile
- No catfishing or impersonation
- Accurate age and information

## BE LEGAL
- No illegal activities
- **NO prostitution or escort services** (instant ban)
- No drugs, weapons, or contraband
- Respect copyright and IP

## PROHIBITED BEHAVIOR
- Spam or scams
- Bot accounts
- Fake profiles
- Payment circumvention
- Sharing purchased content
- Manipulation of metrics
- Extortion or blackmail

## NSFW CONTENT
- Allowed ONLY for verified 18+ users
- Must be properly tagged
- No illegal content
- Platform reserves right to remove

## CALENDAR MEETINGS
- Social purposes ONLY
- Public venues recommended
- NO sexual services
- Verification required

## CREATOR RESPONSIBILITIES
- Professional conduct
- Timely responses
- Quality content
- Fair pricing
- Accurate descriptions

## REPORTING
Report violations via:
- In-app report button
- abuse@avalo.app
- 24/7 support

## CONSEQUENCES
- Warning (first minor violation)
- Suspension (repeat violations)
- Permanent ban (severe violations)
- Legal action (illegal content)

## CONTACT
support@avalo.app
