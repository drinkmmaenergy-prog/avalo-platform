## 1. PURPOSE & SCOPE

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines prohibited behaviors and content on the Avalo platform. This policy applies to all users—including free users, premium subscribers, and creators—across all  features, services, and communications.

**Violation of this AUP may result in:**
- Content removal
- Feature restrictions
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Forfeiture of tokens and earnings
- Reporting to law enforcement authorities

---

## 2. CORE PRINCIPLES

Avalo is committed to providing a platform that is:

✅ **Safe** — Free from harm, abuse, and illegal activity  
✅ **Respectful** — Dignified treatment of all users  
✅ **Legal** — Compliant with applicable laws and regulations  
✅ **Consensual** — Adult interactions based on mutual consent  
✅ **Authentic** — Honest representation and genuine connections

## 3. PROHIBITED CONTENT

### 3.1 Illegal Content

**ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED (ZERO TOLERANCE):**

#### 3.1.1 Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
- Any sexual or suggestive content involving minors (under 18)
- Age-played or simulated minor content
- Grooming behavior or solicitation of minors
- Links to external CSAM resources

**Action:** Immediate permanent ban + law enforcement report + NCMEC reporting

#### 3.1.2 Non-Consensual Intimate Images (NCII)
- "Revenge porn" or leaked intimate content
- Photos/videos shared without subject's consent
- Deepfake pornography without consent
- Hidden camera or voyeuristic content
- Upskirt, downblouse, or similar non-consensual imagery

**Action:** Content removal + account suspension/ban + potential legal action

#### 3.1.3 Human Trafficking & Exploitation
- Promotion of forced labor or sex trafficking
- Content depicting non-consensual situations
- Exploitation of vulnerable individuals
- Coercion or manipulation for sexual purposes

**Action:** Permanent ban + law enforcement cooperation

#### 3.1.4 Violent & Extreme Content
- Graphic violence, gore, or death
- Content promoting terrorism or violent extremism
- Instructions for making weapons or explosives
- Glorification of mass violence or shootings
- Self-harm or suicide content

**Action:** Content removal + escalation to authorities if threat assessment warrants

#### 3.1.5 Illegal Goods & Services
- Sale of illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Unlicensed firearms, ammunition, or weapons
- Stolen goods or counterfeit products
- Prescription medications without authorization
- Endangered species or protected wildlife

**Action:** Content removal + account suspension + law enforcement report

### 3.2 Sexual Content & Adult Services

#### 3.2.1 STRICTLY PROHIBITED — Sexual Services for Money

**ZERO TOLERANCE FOR:**
- **Prostitution** — Offering or soliciting sexual acts for money
- **Escorting** — Advertising companionship with implied sexual services
- **"Donations" for sex** — Any euphemism for paid sexual activity
- **Full-service sex work** — Explicit or coded solicitations
- **Massage parlors** offering "extras"
- **Sugar baby arrangements** with sexual expectations
- **"Girlfriend experience" (GFE)** with sexual components

**Prohibited Terms & Euphemisms:**
- "Full service", "GFE", "PSE", "happy ending"
- "Roses", "donations" (in sexual context)
- "Incall", "outcall" (in sexual service context)
- "No Rushing", "Greek" "French" (sexual service codes)
- "Screening required" (escort terminology)
- Any coded language for sexual services

**Enforcement:**
- AI detection of banned terms
- Manual moderation review
- Instant permanent ban upon confirmation
- Forfeiture of all tokens and earnings
- Cooperation with law enforcement where applicable

**Important:** Avalo **Calendar Bookings** are SOLELY for legitimate social meetings (coffee, dinner, public events). Any attempt to use Calendar for sexual services results in immediate permanent ban.

#### 3.2.2 Adult Content (Allowed with Restrictions)

**PERMITTED (for verified 18+ users):**
- Artistic nudity
- Educational sexual content
- Adult entertainment (solo content)
- Consensual explicit content between verified adults
- NSFW photos, videos, stories

**REQUIREMENTS:**
- Mandatory NSFW tagging
- Blur filters applied automatically
- Age verification (18+) before viewing
- Consent of all participants documented
- No illegal acts depicted

**NOT PERMITTED:**
- Content depicting illegal acts
- Bestiality or zoophilia
- Incest (real or simulated)
- Necrophilia
- Extreme violence or torture
- Fecal content (scat)
- Content involving bodily fluids in extreme or degrading manner

## 6. ENFORCEMENT & MODERATION

### 6.1 Detection Methods

**We use:**
- AI-powered content moderation (text, image, video)
- Keyword and pattern detection
- User reports and flagging
- Manual review by human moderators
- Behavioral analysis and fraud detection

### 6.2 Moderation Process

**Automated Flagging:**
- AI flags suspicious content for review
- High-confidence violations = immediate action
- Low-confidence cases = human review

**Human Review:**
- All permanent bans reviewed by human moderators
- Context considered in borderline cases
- Appeals handled within 48-72 hours

**User Reports:**
- 3+ reports trigger priority review
- Verified reports lead to faster action
- False reporting may result in penalties

---

### 6.3 Appeal Process

**If your content was removed or account suspended:**

1. **Submit Appeal:**
   - Email: appeals@avalo.app
   - Include: User ID, content reference, explanation
   - Attach evidence if applicable

2. **Review Timeline:**
   - Urgent (safety): 24 hours
   - Standard: 3-5 business days
   - Complex: up to 10 business days

3. **Possible Outcomes:**
   - Reversal: Content restored, restrictions lifted
   - Upheld: Original decision stands
   - Modified: Partial restrictions or warning

**No Appeal for:**
- CSAM violations
- Sexual service solicitations
- Violent threats or terrorism
- Fraud or serious financial crimes

---

### 6.4 Penalties & Sanctions

**Graduated Enforcement:**

| Violation Severity | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense |
|-------------------|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Minor** (spam, minor ToS) | Warning | 7-day suspension | 30-day suspension |
| **Moderate** (harassment, hate speech) | 7-day suspension | 30-day suspension | Permanent ban |
| **Severe** (CSAM, sexual services, violence) | Permanent ban | N/A | N/A |

**Sanctions Include:**
- Content removal
- Feature restrictions (messaging, posting, earnings)
- Temporary suspension (7, 30, 90 days)
- Permanent ban
- Forfeiture of tokens and earnings
- Law enforcement reporting (where applicable)
- Legal action (for fraud, illegal activity)
