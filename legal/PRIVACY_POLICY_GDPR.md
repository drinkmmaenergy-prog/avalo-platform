## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Active Accounts
- Data retained while account active
- Unused accounts: 2-year inactivity triggers deletion notice

### 6.2 Deleted Accounts
- 90-day grace period for account recovery
- After 90 days: permanent deletion EXCEPT:
  - Transaction records (7 years - legal requirement)
  - CSAM reports (permanent)
  - Ongoing legal holds

### 6.3 Individual Data Types
- **Chat messages:** Deleted with account (except reported content)
- **Photos:** Deleted with account
- **Transactions:** 7 years
- **Verification data:** 5 years
- **Abuse reports:** 2 years after resolution

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

### 7.1 Right to Access (Art. 15)
- Request copy of your personal data
- Response within 30 days
- First request free, subsequent may incur reasonable fee

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
- Correct inaccurate data 
- Complete incomplete data
- Update via in-app settings or support request

### 7.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17)
- "Right to be forgotten"
- Request full account deletion
- Exceptions for legal obligations (transactions, abuse reports)

### 7.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18)
- Limit processing while verifying accuracy
- Preserve data without processing

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
- Receive your data in structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
- Transfer to another service

### 7.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
- Object to processing for legitimate interests
- Object to marketing (automatic opt-out)

### 7.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)
- Right not to be subject to solely automated decisions
- Our AI moderation and matching include human oversight for significant decisions

### 7.8 Right to Lodge Complaint
- Contact your national Data Protection Authority
- Poland: Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO)
- EU DPA directory: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en
