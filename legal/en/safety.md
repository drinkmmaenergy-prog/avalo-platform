## 2. MEETING SAFETY (SAFEMEET)

### 2.1 BEFORE meeting
‚úÖ **Mandatory:**
1. **Get to know person online** - min. 3-7 days of chat/calls
2. **Video call** - make sure person is real
3. **Check reviews** - if they have bad reviews, reconsider meeting
4. **Public place** - caf√©, restaurant, park during day
5. **Inform someone** - friend or family:
   - Where you're going
   - Who you're meeting (show profile)
   - When you'll be back
6. **Share location** - live with friend
7. **Escape call** - arrange for friend to call at specific time

‚úÖ **Recommended:**
- Read person's social media profiles (if shared)
- Meet during day, not evening (first meeting)
- Plan short meeting (60-90 min) - easier to leave
- Tell friend safety code word via SMS

### 2.2 DURING meeting
‚úÖ **Safety rules:**
- **Own transportation** - arrive and leave alone
- **Public places** - don't go anywhere secluded
- **Stay sober** - alcohol reduces vigilance (first meeting)
- **Don't leave drink** - take it with you to bathroom
- **Phone charged** - full battery and signal
- **Observe behavior** - does it match chat?

‚úÖ **Trust your instinct:**
- If something's off - leave
- You don't have to be polite at all costs
- You can say "I feel uncomfortable, ending meeting"
- Your safety > other person's feelings

‚ùå **DON'T DO (first meeting):**
- Don't go to their home
- Don't invite to your home
- Don't get in their car
- Don't accept "ride home"
- Don't go to isolated places
- Don't meet late at night

### 2.3 AFTER meeting
‚úÖ **What to do:**
- Notify friend you're back safely
- Leave honest review (helps others)
- If it was bad - report to moderation
- If it was great - arrange next meeting!

üö® **If something went wrong:**
- Report to Avalo: abuse@avalo.app
- If crime occurred - police (911/112)
- If you feel threatened - don't hesitate to call for help

## 3. RECOGNIZING THREATS

### 3.1 Grooming (manipulation)
üö© **Warning signs:**
- Excessive attention and compliments ("love bombing")
- Isolating you from friends/family
- Quick progression to intimacy
- Emotional manipulation (guilt, fear)
- Requests for secrets ("just between us")
- Gradually crossing boundaries

### 3.2 Stalking and harassment
üö© **Warning signs:**
- Constant messages despite "no"
- Shows up where you are
- Contact attempts through different platforms
- Threats or intimidation
- Gathering information about you
- Contacting your friends/family

üõ°Ô∏è **What to do:**
1. Clearly say "no" - one message
2. Block person
3. Don't respond to further contact attempts
4. Document everything (screenshots)
5. Report to Avalo
6. If escalates - police

### 3.3 Blackmail and extortion
üö© **Situations:**
- "I'll send your photos to family"
- "I'll publish our conversations"
- "I found your address"
- "Pay or..."

üõ°Ô∏è **What to do:**
1. **DON'T pay** - won't solve problem
2. Stay calm and don't panic
3. Document threats (screenshots)
4. Report immediately to Avalo: abuse@avalo.app
5. Report to police
6. Don't delete evidence
