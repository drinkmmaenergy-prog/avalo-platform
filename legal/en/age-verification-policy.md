## Enforcement and Moderation Process

### 1. Initial Registration Verification

#### Step 1: Age Declaration
During account creation:
- User declares birth date
- System calculates age
- Users under 18 automatically rejected
- Users 18+ proceed to next verification stage

#### Step 2: Basic Verification (All Users)
Required for basic platform access:
- Email verification
- Phone number verification (SMS code)
- Selfie upload with liveness detection
- Initial AI age estimation from selfie
- Agreement to Terms and age requirements

**Processing Time:** 1-5 minutes (automated)

#### Step 3: Enhanced Verification (For Monetization)
Required before using paid features:
- Government-issued ID upload (passport, driver's license, national ID)
- Second selfie holding ID document
- Liveness detection (blink, turn head, smile)
- Document authenticity check (security features, formatting)
- Face matching between selfie and ID photo
- Name and date of birth extraction and validation

**Processing Time:** 2-15 minutes (mostly automated, manual review if flagged)

#### Verification Decision
- **Approved:** Full platform access granted
- **Pending:** Additional documentation requested (occurs in ~8% of cases)
- **Rejected:** Access denied, reason provided, appeal available

### 3. Biometric Verification

#### Liveness Detection
During selfie capture:
- Automated prompts for specific movements or expressions
- Analysis of micro-movements indicating live person
- Detection of photo, video, or mask use attempts
- 3D depth analysis to prevent printout spoofing
- Rapid sequential captures to verify real-time capture

#### Facial Matching
AI compares:
- Your verification selfie
- Your ID document photo
- Your profile photos
- Aging algorithms for older ID documents
- Confidence score >85% required for auto-approval

#### Age Estimation
Biometric systems analyze:
- Facial structure and proportions
- Skin texture and appearance
- Depth and prominence of facial features
- Statistical models based on age databases
- Flags accounts with estimated age <21 for enhanced review

### 4. Continuous Monitoring

#### Post-Verification Surveillance
After initial approval:
- AI monitors profile photos for age inconsistencies
- Behavioral analysis for patterns suggesting minor activity
- Scans content uploads for indicators of underage presence
- Flags accounts with reports from other users
- Cross-references account data with growing fraud databases

#### Triggers for Re-Verification
Accounts are flagged for re-verification if:
- Profile photos suggest significantly younger appearance
- Behavioral patterns consistent with minor usage
- Multiple user reports of suspected underage activity
- Unusual account activity patterns
- Device or network indicators of shared family use
- Previously failed verification attempts from same device/IP
- Significant updates to profile information

#### Re-Verification Process
If flagged:
1. Account placed in restricted mode (cannot use paid features)
2. User receives notification to complete re-verification within 7 days
3. More stringent verification process applied
4. Manual review by trained specialist
5. Decision within 48-72 hours
6. Account restoration or permanent ban
