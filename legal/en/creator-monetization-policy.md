## Platform Safety Principles

### 1. Authentic Monetization
Avalo supports genuine monetization of:
- Your personality and social presence
- Your time and attention in conversations
- Original content you create
- Real-world meetups in safe public settings
- Educational or entertainment value you provide

We strictly prohibit monetization of:
- Sexual services or prostitution
- Illegal activities or goods
- Deceptive or fraudulent schemes
- Content you don't own or have rights to
- Other users' personal information

### 2. Fair Value Exchange
Every transaction must represent fair value:
- Clear communication of what's being offered
- Delivery of promised services or content
- Honest representation of yourself and offerings
- Reasonable pricing aligned with value provided
- No bait-and-switch or misleading tactics

### 3. User Protection
Creator monetization must not:
- Exploit vulnerable users
- Use manipulative psychological tactics
- Create financial hardship for fans
- Violate consent or boundaries
- Engage in predatory behavior

### 4. Platform Integrity
To maintain trust:
- No circumventing platform payment systems
- No artificial inflation of metrics or engagement
- No collusion to manipulate rankings
- No abuse of refund or chargeback systems
- Honest reporting of income for tax purposes

### 5. Legal Compliance
All monetization must:
- Comply with local, state, and federal laws
- Meet tax reporting requirements
- Follow anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Adhere to consumer protection standards

## Jurisdiction and Legal Compliance

### Tax Compliance

#### United States Creators
üìã **Requirements:**
- Provide valid Social Security Number (SSN) or Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- Complete Form W-9 upon reaching monetization threshold
- Receive Form 1099-NEC if earning $600+ annually
- Report earnings on Schedule C (self-employment income)
- Pay quarterly estimated taxes if applicable
- Self-employment tax applies (15.3% Social Security/Medicare)

#### International Creators
üìã **Requirements:**
- Complete Form W-8BEN (non-US individuals)
- Provide valid tax identification from home country
- Subject to 30% US withholding (or lower treaty rate)
- Must comply with home country tax laws
- Responsible for currency conversion reporting
- May need to register as business in home jurisdiction

#### Tax Reporting Thresholds
- US Creators: 1099 issued if $600+ earned annually
- Non-US Creators: 1042-S issued for withheld amounts
- All creators responsible for reporting regardless of 1099 receipt

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

#### Know Your Customer (KYC)
Required for all Creators earning money:
- Government-issued photo ID verification
- Selfie with liveness detection
- Address verification (utility bill or bank statement)
- Social Security Number or equivalent
- Background check for high-earning accounts ($10K+ monthly)

#### Transaction Monitoring
We monitor for:
- Large or frequent withdrawals
- Rapid deposit and withdrawal patterns
- Geographic anomalies
- Suspicious transaction structuring
- Flagged accounts or blacklisted individuals

#### Reporting Obligations
We report to authorities:
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) to FinCEN
- Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) over $10,000
- Identified money laundering patterns
- Sanctioned individuals or entities

### Consumer Protection Laws

#### Federal Trade Commission (FTC)
Must comply with:
- Truth in advertising standards
- Clear and conspicuous disclosures
- Prohibition on deceptive practices
- Endorsement and testimonial rules (if applicable)
- Cooling-off period for certain sales

#### State Laws
Variable by jurisdiction:
- Consumer fraud and protection acts
- Service contract regulations
- Deceptive trade practices laws
- Age of majority for contracts
- Required business registrations

### Intellectual Property

#### Copyright (DMCA)
- We comply with Digital Millennium Copyright Act
- Takedown process for copyright violations

- Repeat infringer policy (3 strikes = permanent ban)
- Safe harbor protections for platform

#### Creator Responsibilities
You must:

- Obtain model releases for recognizable people
- Respect trademark rights
- Not infringe patents or trade secrets
- Respond to valid takedown notices

### International Considerations
