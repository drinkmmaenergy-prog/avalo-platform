## 3. DATA SHARING

### 3.1 Basic Principle
**WE DO NOT SELL YOUR PERSONAL DATA.** We will never sell, rent, or share your data for marketing purposes with third parties.

### 3.2 Payment Processors
We share necessary data with:
- **Stripe** – card payment processing
- **PayPal** – PayPal payment processing
- Data: name, email, transaction amount (encrypted)

### 3.3 Cloud Providers
Data hosting on:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** – primary hosting
- **Google Cloud Platform** – backup and redundancy
- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit

### 3.4 Analytics Providers
- **Firebase Analytics** – mobile app analytics (anonymized)
- **Mixpanel** – user behavior analysis (anonymized)
- Data collected in aggregated form, without personal identification

### 3.5 Security Tools
- **Cloudflare** – DDoS protection
- **PhotoDNA** – CSAM detection
- **Sift Science** – fraud detection

### 3.6 Law Enforcement
We share data only when required by law:
- Court order
- Prosecutor's summons
- Police inquiry in criminal matters
- We log all such requests

## 4. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

### 4.1 Right to Access Data
You can request a copy of all your data. We respond within 30 days.

### 4.2 Right to Rectification
You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data at any time.

### 4.3 Right to Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
You can request account and data deletion. Exceptions:
- Transaction data (required by law for 7 years)
- Data in ongoing legal proceedings
- Data necessary to protect our rights

### 4.4 Right to Data Portability
You can receive your data in JSON or CSV format and transfer it elsewhere.

### 4.5 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for marketing data processing at any time.

### 4.6 Right to Restrict Processing
You can request restriction of your data processing in certain situations.

### 4.7 Right to Object to Marketing
You can unsubscribe from newsletter and marketing notifications at any time.

### 4.8 GDPR Contact
Email: privacy@avalo.app
Data Protection Officer: dpo@avalo.app
We respond within 30 days in accordance with GDPR.

## 5. DATA RETENTION

### 5.1 Active Account
Data stored while account is active and for 90 days after last activity.

### 5.2 Deleted Account
After account deletion:
- Most data deleted within 30 days
- Transaction data stored for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Backups deleted after maximum 90 days

### 5.3 Transaction Data
Stored for 7 years in accordance with tax and financial regulations.

### 5.4 Backup
- Backup created daily
- Stored for maximum 90 days
- Fully encrypted

### 5.5 System Logs
- Stored for 12 months
- Used only for debugging and security
- Automatically deleted after one year

## 7. SECURITY

### 7.1 End-to-End Encryption
- All private chats are E2E encrypted
- We have no access to message content
- We use Signal protocol

### 7.2 HTTPS for All Communication
All communication between you and servers is HTTPS encrypted (TLS 1.3).

### 7.3 Hashed Passwords
- Passwords never stored in plain text
- We use bcrypt with high cost
- Passwords are salted and hashed

### 7.4 Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Available for all users:
- SMS
- Authenticator app
- Email backup

### 7.5 Regular Security Audits
- Security audit every 6 months
- Penetration testing
- Bug bounty program

### 7.6 24/7 Threat Monitoring
- Automatic anomaly detection
- Real-time alerts
- On-call security team

### 7.7 Breach Reporting
In case of data breach:
- Notification within 72 hours (GDPR)
- Email to all users
- Cooperation with authorities
