## 1. INTRODUCTION

This document discloses how Avalo uses Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and automated decision-making systems across the platform. We are committed to transparency regarding automated processing that may significantly affect your experience or rights.

## 2. LEGAL BASIS FOR AI PROCESSING

Under **GDPR Article 6(1)** and **Article 9(2)**, we process data through AI systems based on:

- **Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f))** — Platform safety, fraud prevention, user experience
- **Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b))** — Matching algorithms, content delivery
- **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a), Art. 9(2)(a))** — Personalization features, NSFW content filtering
- **Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))** — CSAM detection, illegal content identification

## 3. AI SYSTEMS DEPLOYED ON AVALO

### 3.1 Content Moderation AI

**Purpose:** Detect prohibited, harmful, or illegal content  
**Technology:** Multi-modal AI (text, image, video analysis)  
**Data Processed:**
- User-uploaded images and videos
- Chat messages and text content
- Profile descriptions and bios
- Comments and reviews

**Automated Decisions:**
- Content flagging for human review
- Temporary content removal pending review
- NSFW content classification
- Detection of prohibited terms (prostitution, drugs, violence)

**Human Oversight:** YES
- All permanent bans reviewed by human moderators
- Users can appeal automated decisions
- False positives corrected within 24-48 hours

**Your Rights:**
- Right to human review (GDPR Art. 22(3))
- Right to contest decision
- Right to explanation

---

### 3.2 Matching & Recommendation AI

**Purpose:** Suggest potential matches and content based on preferences  
**Technology:** Collaborative filtering, embeddings, vector similarity  
**Data Processed:**
- Profile information (age, location, interests)
- Swipe history and interactions
- Chat engagement patterns
- Content preferences

**Automated Decisions:**
- Profile suggestions in discovery feed
- "You might like" recommendations
- Content feed prioritization
- Search result ranking

**Human Oversight:** NO (low-risk decisions)  
**Impact:** Low — affects content shown but does not restrict access

**Your Rights:**
- Algorithm transparency (general principles disclosed)
- Personalization can be disabled in Settings
- No legal or similarly significant effect

---

### 3.3 Fraud Detection & Trust Scoring

**Purpose:** Identify suspicious behavior, protect users from scams  
**Technology:** Behavioral analysis, anomaly detection, multi-factor trust scoring  
**Data Processed:**
- Transaction patterns
- Login behavior (device, location, time)
- Chat patterns and content
- User reports and violations
- Financial activity

**Automated Decisions:**
- Risk scores for transactions
- Temporary account restrictions (high-risk behavior)
- Enhanced verification requirements
- Payment holds for fraud investigation

**Human Oversight:** YES for significant actions
- Account suspensions reviewed within 24 hours
- Payment holds reviewed by finance team
- Users can request manual review

**Your Rights:**
- Right to explanation of trust score factors
- Right to contest restrictions
- Right to human review before permanent action

---

### 3.4 Age Verification AI

**Purpose:** Ensure all users are 18+ years old  
**Technology:** Liveness detection, age estimation from facial analysis  
**Data Processed:**
- Live selfie images
- Government ID documents (encrypted)
- Biometric data (age markers)

**Automated Decisions:**
- Age estimation (18+ confirmation)
- Identity document authenticity verification
- Liveness detection (not a photo or mask)

**Human Oversight:** YES for edge cases
- Uncertain results (confidence <95%) reviewed manually
- Suspected minors escalated immediately
- False rejections can be appealed

**Your Rights:**
- Right to alternative verification methods
- Right to human review if rejected
- Right to deletion of verification data after confirmation

---

### 3.5 NSFW & Sexual Content Classification

**Purpose:** Enforce age restrictions, content labeling, safe browsing  
**Technology:** Image recognition, explicit content detection  
**Data Processed:**
- Photos and videos uploaded by creators
- Profile images
- Chat media

**Automated Decisions:**
- NSFW tagging (Safe, Moderate, Explicit)

- Artistic nudity vs explicit content reviewed manually

**Your Rights:**
- Right to contest NSFW classification
- Right to request manual review
- NSFW viewing preferences controlled in Settings

## 4. HIGH-RISK AI SYSTEMS (EU AI ACT COMPLIANCE)

Under **EU AI Act Article 6**, the following are classified as **High-Risk AI Systems**:

### 4.1 Content Moderation (Art. 6, Annex III)
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Compliance Measures:**
  - Human oversight for all permanent actions
  - Appeal mechanisms (response within 48 hours)
  - Regular bias testing
  - Accuracy audits (quarterly)
  - Transparency reports published

### 4.2 Age Verification (Art. 6, Annex III)
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Compliance Measures:**
  - Alternative human-based verification available
  - Regular accuracy testing (>99% target)
  - Bias mitigation (age, ethnicity, gender)
  - Data minimization (biometric data deleted after verification)

### 4.3 Fraud Detection
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Compliance Measures:**
  - Human review before account termination
  - Weekly model performance monitoring
  - False positive rate tracking (<2%)
  - Regular retraining with diverse data

## 5. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR ARTICLE 22

You have the right **NOT to be subject to solely automated decision-making**, including profiling, which produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you.

### 5.1 When Automated Decisions Occur

We use automated decision-making for:
- Content moderation (flagging, temporary removal)
- Fraud detection (temporary restrictions)
- Age verification (access denial)

### 5.2 How We Safeguard Your Rights

✅ **Human Review:** All significant decisions reviewed by humans  
✅ **Right to Explanation:** Request explanation of any automated decision  
✅ **Right to Contest:** Appeal any automated decision  
✅ **Right to Human Review:** Request human intervention

### 5.3 Exercising Your Rights

**To request human review or explanation:**
1. Email: ai-decisions@avalo.app
2. Subject: "GDPR Article 22 Request"
3. Include: Your user ID, decision reference, reason for appeal

**Response Time:** 72 hours for urgent safety decisions, 30 days for others

## 6. AI TRANSPARENCY & EXPLAINABILITY

### 6.1 How AI Decisions Are Made

**Content Moderation:**
- AI scans content for prohibited patterns (violence, CSAM, hate speech)
- Confidence score assigned (0-100%)
- High confidence (>90%) = automated action
- Low confidence (<90%) = human review

**Matching Algorithm:**
- User preferences weighted (age range, location, interests)
- Behavioral signals (swipe history, engagement)
- Similarity scoring between profiles
- Randomization to prevent filter bubbles

**Trust Scoring:**
- Positive signals: verified identity, consistent behavior, positive reviews
- Negative signals: reports, disputes, unusual patterns
- Score range: 0-1000 (higher = more trusted)
- Thresholds for restrictions disclosed upon request

### 6.2 Limitations of AI

We acknowledge that AI systems:
- Can make mistakes (false positives/negatives)
- May exhibit unintended biases
- Cannot fully understand context or nuance
- Require continuous monitoring and improvement
