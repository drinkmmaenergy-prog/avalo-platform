## OVERVIEW

This pack replaces the previous "PG-safe" notification tone with notifications designed to drive dating engagement through psychological triggers: recognition, jealousy, urgency, and FOMO.

**Key Change:** Shift from passive, safe messaging to active, emotionally-driven notifications that encourage immediate action.

---

## NOTIFICATION TYPES

### 1. Recognition / Ego Boost

**Purpose:** Validate user attractiveness and encourage confidence

**Examples:**
- "Someone is totally into your vibe — take control of the moment."
- "They're drawn to your energy — don't leave them waiting."
- "Your profile caught someone's attention — make your move."
- "Someone can't stop thinking about you — show them what they're missing."

**Psychological Trigger:** Ego validation, empowerment, confidence boost

---

### 2. Jealousy & Urgency

**Purpose:** Create competitive tension and fear of losing opportunity

**Examples:**
- "Your match is getting attention from others — don't lose the upper hand."
- "They're talking to someone else right now — remind them why you're worth it."
- "Other people are noticing your match — time to make your claim."
- "Competition is heating up — don't let someone else win."

**Psychological Trigger:** Loss aversion, competitive drive, territorial instinct

---

### 3. Miss-Me Trigger

**Purpose:** Leverage curiosity and implied interest

**Examples:**
- "He checked your profile twice. Feels like he's waiting."
- "She keeps coming back to your photos — don't leave her hanging."
- "They viewed your story 3 times today — curiosity is building."
- "Someone's been quietly watching you — time to break the silence."

**Psychological Trigger:** Curiosity, flattery, reciprocity impulse

---

### 4. FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

**Purpose:** Create time-sensitive urgency

**Examples:**
- "A perfect match is online right now — timing is everything."
- "Your ideal match is active for the next 10 minutes — don't miss this."
- "Someone who matches your type just joined — be the first to connect."
- "This window won't last — catch them while they're available."

**Psychological Trigger:** Scarcity, urgency, opportunity cost

## TONE GUIDELINES

### ✅ REQUIRED TONE
- Confident and direct
- Emotionally engaging
- Action-oriented
- Seductive but tasteful
- Creates urgency and desire
- Empowering to the user

### ❌ PROHIBITED TONE
- Passive language
- Sterile or clinical wording
- Overly formal communication
- "Friendship only" messaging
- Moralizing about flirting
- Weak, tentative phrasing
- Generic, boring notifications

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Notification Delivery Strategy
1. **Timing:** Send during peak engagement hours (evenings, weekends)
2. **Frequency:** Balance between urgency and notification fatigue
3. **Personalization:** Use user's preferences and behavior patterns
4. **A/B Testing:** Continuously test message variations for engagement

### Success Metrics
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Time to action after notification
- Conversation initiation rate
- Response rate within 1 hour
- User retention after notification engagement

### Compliance Considerations
- Ensure notifications don't trigger spam filters
- Respect user notification preferences
- Allow opt-out for specific notification types
- Maintain platform guidelines compliance
- Balance persuasion with user comfort
