**Goals:** Meet locals abroad, cultural experiences, romantic adventures

**Profile:** Nightlife regular, social butterfly, loves attention  
**Goals:** VIP event access, paid party companionship, social monetization  
**Uses:** Event features, Stories, booking system, premium chat

**Profile:** Divorced or long-term single, ready to date again  
**Goals:** Find genuine connections, navigate modern dating safely  
**Uses:** Verification features, safe messaging, gradual engagement
