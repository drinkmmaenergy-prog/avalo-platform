```markdown
# How Paid Chat Works

Paid chat allows you to have meaningful conversations with interesting people while supporting their time and attention.

## Message Pricing

- Each message sent costs tokens
- Token prices are set by the recipient
- You see the cost before sending any message
- View pricing in the chat header

## Sending Messages

1. Open a chat with someone
2. Check their per-message token price
3. Type your message
4. Confirm payment before sending
5. Message is delivered instantly

## Refund Policy

You can request a refund if:
- The other person doesn't reply within 24 hours
- You sent the first message in the conversation
- The refund request is made within 48 hours

### How to Request a Refund

1. Go to chat details
2. Tap "Request Refund"
3. Select the messages
4. Submit your request

Refunds are processed within 48 hours.

## Payment Methods

- Use your token balance
- Purchase tokens via in-app wallet
- Accepted payment methods: Credit card, Google Pay, Apple Pay

## Safety Tips

- Never share sensitive personal information
- Report inappropriate behavior immediately
- Use the panic button if you feel unsafe
- Block users who make you uncomfortable

## Need Help?

If you have questions about paid chat, [contact our support team](/support/tickets/create).
```

### 2. Calendar Meetings and Refunds

**Article ID:** `calendar-meetings-refunds`  
**Category:** `CALENDAR_AND_MEETINGS`  
**Slug:** `calendar-meetings-refunds`  
**Tags:** `["calendar", "meetings", "booking", "refunds"]`

**Title (EN):** Calendar Meetings & Refund Policy  
**Title (PL):** Spotkania kalendarzowe i polityka zwrotów

**Short Summary (EN):** Understand how to book calendar meetings and when refunds are available.

**Short Summary (PL):** Dowiedz się, jak zarezerwować spotkania kalendarzowe i kiedy dostępne są zwroty.

**Body (EN):**
```markdown
# Calendar Meetings & Refund Policy

Book one-on-one meetings with interesting people through our calendar system.

## How Booking Works

1. Browse available time slots
2. Select date and time
3. Pay the booking fee
4. Receive confirmation
5. Meet at the scheduled time

## Meeting Types

- **Video calls** - Virtual face-to-face meetings
- **In-person meetups** - Physical meetings at agreed locations
- **Phone calls** - Voice-only conversations

## Pricing

- Prices set by the meeting host
- Includes duration and meeting type
- Payment required upfront
- Platform takes 20% service fee

## Refund Policy

### Full Refunds Available If:

- Meeting cancelled by host
- Cancellation made 24+ hours before meeting
- Technical issues prevent the meeting
- Host doesn't show up

### Partial Refunds (50%) If:

- Cancellation made 12-24 hours before meeting
- Rescheduled by mutual agreement

### No Refunds If:

- You cancel less than 12 hours before
- You don't show up ("no-show")
- You're more than 15 minutes late

## How to Cancel

1. Go to "My Bookings"
2. Select the meeting
3. Tap "Cancel Booking"
4. Provide reason
5. Confirm cancellation

Refunds processed within 3-5 business days.

## Safety Guidelines

- Meet in public places (for in-person)
- Share meeting details with a friend
- Use panic button if needed
- Report any safety concerns

## Need Help?

Questions about bookings? [Contact support](/support/tickets/create).
```

As a content creator, you earn money through paid chats, calendar bookings, and events.

- No hidden fees

### Paid Chat & Calls

- You're responsible for taxes
- Platform provides earnings reports
- Consult with tax professional

- Bank transfer (SEPA)
- PayPal (coming soon)
- Crypto (coming soon)

Payout issues? [Contact support](/support/tickets/create).

### 6. Safety & Panic Button

**Article ID:** `safety-panic-button`  
**Category:** `SAFETY_AND_REPORTING`  
**Slug:** `safety-panic-button`  
**Tags:** `["safety", "panic", "emergency", "reporting"]`

**Title (EN):** Safety Features & Panic Button  
**Title (PL):** Funkcje bezpieczeństwa i przycisk paniki

**Short Summary (EN):** Learn how to stay safe, use the panic button, and report concerning behavior.

**Short Summary (PL):** Dowiedz się, jak zachować bezpieczeństwo, używać przycisku paniki i zgłaszać niepokojące zachowania.

**Body (EN):**
```markdown
# Safety Features & Panic Button

Your safety is our top priority. Here's how to stay safe on Avalo.

## Panic Button

Emergency feature for dangerous situations.

### When to Use
- You feel physically threatened
- Someone is following you
- Emergency situation during meetup
- Immediate danger

### What It Does
1. Alerts trusted contacts
2. Shares your location
3. Notifies platform safety team
4. Records incident details

### How to Activate
- Press and hold panic button (3 seconds)
- Confirm emergency
- Help is on the way

### Trusted Contacts
- Add 2-3 trusted friends/family
- They receive instant alerts
- Can track your location
- Can contact authorities

## Reporting Users

Report inappropriate behavior:
1. Go to user profile
2. Tap "Report"
3. Select reason
4. Provide details
5. Submit report

Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

## Block Users

Block anyone making you uncomfortable:
1. Go to their profile
2. Tap three dots (...)
3. Select "Block"
4. Confirm

Blocked users cannot:
- Message you
- See your profile
- Find you in discovery

## Safety Tips

### For Meetings
- Meet in public places
- Tell someone your plans
- Share location with friend
- Trust your instincts

### For Chat
- Don't share sensitive info
- Avoid financial requests
- Report suspicious behavior
- Keep conversations in-app

### For Events
- Stay with groups
- Don't accept drinks from strangers
- Have an exit plan
- Keep phone charged

## Need Immediate Help?

- **Emergency:** Call local police (112 in EU)
- **Safety concerns:** [Contact support](/support/tickets/create)
- **Crisis helpline:** Available in app settings

Remember: It's okay to leave any situation where you feel unsafe.
```
