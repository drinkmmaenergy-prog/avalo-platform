# Privacy Policy

**Version 1.0.0**  
**Effective Date: December 13, 2024**

## 1. Data Controller

**Avalo**  
Contact: privacy@avalo.app

## 2. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Contract**: To provide platform services
- **Consent**: For optional features (marketing, location sharing)
- **Legitimate Interest**: Fraud prevention, safety, platform improvement

## 3. Data Categories We Collect

### Profile Data
- Name, age, gender, sexual orientation
- Photos and bio
- Preferences and interests

### Communication Data
- Messages metadata (not content unless reported)
- Call logs (duration, participants)
- Video/voice call quality metrics

### Location Data
- Approximate location for matching (with consent)
- Precise location for safety during meetings (optional)

### Payment Data
- Transaction history
- Token balance
- Payment method tokens (via Stripe/Apple/Google)

### Verification Data
- Selfie for age verification
- Government ID (if enhanced verification requested)

### Usage Data
- Device information
- App activity and interactions
- Crash reports and diagnostics

## 4. Processing Purposes

We use your data to:
- **Matching & Discovery**: Show you potential connections
- **Safety**: Verify age, detect fraud, enforce policies
- **Support**: Respond to tickets and disputes
- **Analytics**: Improve features and user experience
- **Legal**: Comply with legal obligations (e.g., CSAM reporting)

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: As long as account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: 30 days for recovery, then permanent deletion
- **Legal holds**: Retained as required by law
- **Messages**: 90 days after chat ends (unless reported)
- **Transaction records**: 7 years for tax/accounting

## 6. Data Sharing

We share data with:

### Service Providers (Processors)
- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: Hosting and database
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Apple/Google**: In-app purchases
- **Analytics providers**: App performance monitoring

### Legal Requirements
- Law enforcement (with valid legal request)
- Child safety organizations (for CSAM reports)

### Business Transfers
- In case of merger or acquisition

**We never sell your personal data.**

## 7. User Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request copy of your data
- **Deletion**: Request account and data deletion
- **Portability**: Export your data
- **Objection**: Object to certain processing
- **Restriction**: Limit processing
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data

To exercise rights: privacy@avalo.app

## 8. International Transfers

Data may be transferred outside your country to:
- Google Cloud servers (global infrastructure)
- Service providers with adequate protections (Standard Contractual Clauses)

## 9. Children's Privacy

**Avalo is 18+ only.** We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Accounts found to belong to minors will be immediately terminated and data deleted.

## 10. Security Measures

We implement:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits
- Incident response procedures

## 11. Cookies & Tracking

We use:
- **Essential cookies**: For authentication and security
- **Analytics**: To understand app usage (with consent)
- **Third-party**: Via integrated services (Firebase, Stripe)

You can manage preferences in Settings.

## 12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes require re-acceptance. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 13. Contact & Complaints

**Questions**: privacy@avalo.app  
**Data Protection Officer**: dpo@avalo.app

**EU Users**: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
