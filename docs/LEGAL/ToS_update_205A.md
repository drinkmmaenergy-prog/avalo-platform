## User Content Policy Update

### Dating, Flirting, and Romance — PACK 207

**Dating, flirting and romance are allowed.** Users may monetize their time, attention and presence. **Explicit sexual content and sexual services are prohibited.**

Avalo is a premium dating & social lifestyle platform where people can flirt, build chemistry and meet — and creators may earn from their time, attention and presence. Romance and attraction are welcome. Nudity and sexual services are prohibited. Age 18+ with identity verification is required.

Avalo allows dating, flirting, romance and emotional expression, including sensual and attractive self-presentation without nudity. Explicit sexual content or sexual services are prohibited. Users may monetize their time, attention and presence but may not offer or request sexual acts. Violations result in permanent account deactivation.

✅ **Dating and Romance**
- Meeting people for romantic relationships

✅ **Creator Monetization**
- Earning from time spent in conversations
- Earning from attention and presence

✅ **Social Lifestyle**
- Nightlife events and social gatherings

## What is Prohibited

❌ **Explicit Sexual Content**
- Nudity or sexually explicit images
- Pornographic content
- Sexual acts on camera
- Explicit sexual messages or requests

❌ **Sexual Services**
- Offering or requesting sexual acts for payment
- Prostitution or escort services
- Any form of sexual transaction
- Solicitation of sexual services

❌ **Deceptive Practices**
- Misleading users about services offered
- False promises or scams
- Impersonation or fake identities
- Fraudulent monetization schemes

## Enforcement

**Violations of this policy will result in:**

1. **First Offense (Severe):** Immediate suspension pending review
2. **Confirmed Violation:** Permanent account deactivation
3. **No Appeals:** Sexual service violations are non-appealable
4. **No Refunds:** Tokens or payments are non-refundable upon violation
