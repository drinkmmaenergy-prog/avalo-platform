## Consent & Respect

- **No means no**: Respect boundaries immediately
- Stop contact when asked
- Don't pressure for personal info or meetings
- Report inappropriate behavior

## Safety Tools

### Report & Block
- Use the report button for violations
- Block users you don't want to interact with
- Reports are reviewed within 24 hours

### Privacy Controls
- Control who can see your profile
- Manage location sharing
- Set communication preferences

## Enforcement

Violations result in:

1. **Warning**: First minor offense
2. **Temporary Freeze**: Repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent Ban**: Serious or repeated violations

We may skip steps for severe violations (CSAM, threats, fraud).

## Appeals

If you believe your account was restricted unfairly:
- Contact: appeals@avalo.app
- Include: User ID, reason, explanation
- Response within 5 business days

## Safety Tips

- Meet in public places first
- Tell a friend your plans
- Trust your instincts
- Don't share financial information
- Use in-app messaging until comfortable

## Reporting CSAM

If you encounter child sexual abuse material:
- Report immediately via in-app button
- We are legally required to report to authorities
- Content will be preserved for investigation

## Updates

We may update these guidelines. Continued use means acceptance.
