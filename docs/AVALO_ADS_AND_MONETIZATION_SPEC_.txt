Avalo Ads & Monetization Specification (v1)
1. Purpose

Avalo uses in-app advertising to monetize free users and increase engagement, without disrupting the paid experience.

2. Ad Platforms

Mobile: Google AdMob SDK

Web: Google Ad Manager / Meta Audience Network

Optional: internal promo banners for token packs or VIP upgrades

3. Placement Overview
Section	Format	Frequency	Condition

Swipe	Interstitial	every 15 swipes	Free users only
Discovery	Small promo tile	1 per scroll batch	Optional
Dashboard (Web)	Inline banner	1 per 3 sections	Free users
4. Targeting & Policy

Ads shown only to non-VIP and non-Royal users.

No personalized ads for minors (18+ only anyway).

Sensitive categories (escort, dating, adult) excluded.

GDPR/CCPA-compliant consent dialog before first ad.
