## Avalo In-App Messaging Tone Guidelines

**FULL OVERWRITE** — Removes previous soft, sterile, PG-friendly tone.

---

## TONE AVALO MUST USE

### Core Attributes:
1. **Sexy but Classy** — Alluring without being explicit
2. **Confident, Not Needy** — Direct and bold, never desperate
3. **Playful & Teasing** — Fun with subtle edge
4. **Luxury Experience** — Premium feel, never cheap
5. **Emotionally Stimulating** — Creates anticipation and desire
6. **Romance-Friendly** — Embraces attraction and connection

## TONE AVALO MUST NEVER USE

### ❌ FORBIDDEN PHRASES & APPROACHES:

1. **PACK 207: All anti-romance language has been permanently removed**
   - These phrases have been eliminated across all touchpoints
   - Dating-aligned, confident language is now the standard

2. **Overly Safe/Sterile Language**
   - ❌ "You have a new connection!"
   - ❌ "Someone viewed your profile."
   - ❌ "Check your matches today."
   - ❌ "New message from a user."

3. **Corporate/Generic Tone**
   - ❌ "Welcome to our platform!"
   - ❌ "Complete your profile to continue."
   - ❌ "Thank you for using our service."
   - ❌ "Your account has been updated."

4. **Desperate/Needy Language**
   - ❌ "Please come back!"
   - ❌ "We miss you!"
   - ❌ "Don't leave us!"
   - ❌ "Why haven't you replied?"

## TONE APPLICATION BY CATEGORY

### 1. First Impressions (Onboarding)
**Goal:** Create excitement about possibilities
- ✅ "Welcome to where chemistry happens."
- ✅ "Your most interesting connections start here."
- ✅ "Get ready to meet your match."
- ❌ "Welcome! Set up your profile to get started."

### 2. Daily Engagement
**Goal:** Drive action with subtle urgency
- ✅ "Tonight's lineup looks promising."
- ✅ "Fresh faces, fresh chances."
- ✅ "Your kind of people are here now."
- ❌ "You have 5 new matches to review."

### 3. Premium/Monetization
**Goal:** Luxury appeal, not scarcity
- ✅ "Unlock the VIP experience."
- ✅ "See who's obsessed with you."
- ✅ "Get access to your biggest admirers."
- ❌ "Upgrade now! Limited time offer!"

### 4. Re-Engagement
**Goal:** Pull back with intrigue, not guilt
- ✅ "Someone's still thinking about you."
- ✅ "Your absence has been noticed."
- ✅ "They're wondering where you went."
- ❌ "We miss you! Come back and chat!"

### 5. Safety/Moderation
**Goal:** Maintain tone even in serious contexts
- ✅ "Keep it classy. They'll appreciate it."
- ✅ "Real attraction doesn't need fake moves."
- ✅ "Quality over quantity wins every time."
- ❌ "Your behavior violates our community guidelines."

## CULTURAL SENSITIVITY

### Maintain Tone Across Cultures:
- **Universal Appeal:** Focus on human attraction dynamics
- **Avoid Cultural Stereotypes:** No region-specific dating clichés
- **Respect Local Norms:** Adjust intensity, not essence
- **Premium Feel:** Luxury transcends borders

### Language-Specific Adaptations:
When translating, preserve:
1. Confidence level
2. Playful edge
3. Romantic undertone
4. Premium positioning

**Example:**
- English: "Make the move before someone else does."
- Spanish: "Haz el movimiento antes que alguien más."
- French: "Agis avant qu'un autre ne le fasse."
- Polish: "Rusz się, zanim ktoś inny to zrobi."

## QUALITY CONTROL

### Red Flags (Automatic Rejection):
- Using "platform," "service," or "app" in user-facing copy
- Any anti-romance messaging
- Corporate jargon or legal-speak
- Desperation indicators ("please," "we miss you")
- Generic dating app clichés

### Green Flags (Approved Tone):
- Creates anticipation or intrigue
- Implies exclusivity or desirability
- Empowers user to take action
- Feels premium and intentional
- Subtle romantic tension
