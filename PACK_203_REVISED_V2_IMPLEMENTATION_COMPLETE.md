## PROHIBITED THEMES — PERMANENT DELETION

The following messaging themes are **INCOMPATIBLE** with Avalo's product and must **NEVER RETURN**:

### ❌ BANNED PHRASES
1. "Romantic behavior is inappropriate"
2. "Flirting is discouraged"
3. "Serious dating not allowed"
4. "Users should avoid sexual tension"
5. Any suggestion that attraction is problematic
6. Any messaging that discourages authentic romantic expression
7. Any language implying romance is unprofessional or unwelcome

### Why These Were Removed
- **Contradicts product purpose:** Avalo is a dating platform
- **Confuses users:** Mixed messaging about platform intent
- **Reduces engagement:** Users seeking romance will leave
- **Wrong positioning:** Treats normal human behavior as problematic
- **Competitor advantage:** Pushes users to platforms that celebrate attraction
