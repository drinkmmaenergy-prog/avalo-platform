## ğŸ¨ Visual Guidelines for Ads

### Image Style
- Dark mode aesthetic with neon accents (#A62EFF, #FF47A3)
- Lifestyle photography (nightlife, restaurants, travel, city vibes)
- Young adults (25-35) looking confident and happy
- Eye contact, smiling, fun energy
- NO nudity, NO lingerie, NO erotic posing

### Video Style
- Premium nightlife b-roll
- People having fun at restaurants/bars
- City lights, urban environments
- Testimonials from real users
- 15-60 second formats

### Logo Usage
- Always use official Avalo logo
- Primary purple (#A62EFF) or white depending on background
- Never distort or recolor
